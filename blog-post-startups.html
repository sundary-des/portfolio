100 AI Startups: what's actually working in 2026

Mar 18, 2026

Key Patterns Across AI Startups

Based on trends across 100 startup listings as of March 2026, this analysis highlights where AI is creating real value — across products, business models, and categories.

Domain Topics:

  • AI is everywhere — ~60% of listings have AI as a core feature, spanning productivity, creative tools, marketing, HR, education, and health
  • HealthTech is the second biggest cluster — calorie trackers, mental wellness, gut health, quit smoking, facial analysis
  • Utility apps are a steady category — cleaners, VPNs, scanners, phone tools — often sold as portfolios

Emerging patterns:

  • AI + creative (music gen, video, headshots, character creators) is a hot exit category
  • Dating/social apps with AI twists are increasing
  • "Profitable but small" — many listings show $20K–$400K ARR with thin teams, signaling solo founders exiting micro-SaaS

Business model split:

  • B2C dominates (~70%) — most mobile apps target consumers with subscriptions
  • B2B is growing — especially in AI automation, recruiting, customer service, and marketing SaaS

Product type:

  • Apps are the majority (~55%) — iOS-first is very common
  • SaaS is strong (~35%) — recurring revenue, often B2B
  • Portfolios appear regularly — sellers bundling 5–14 apps together
